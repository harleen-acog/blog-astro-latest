## 🎯 What is Deep Work?

Deep work is the ability to focus without distraction on a cognitively demanding task.

It’s the difference between:
- Shallow work → replying to emails, scrolling, multitasking  
- Deep work → building, thinking, solving hard problems  

Most meaningful results come from deep work, not busy work.

---

## 🧠 Why Focus is Becoming Rare

We live in a world designed for distraction:

- Constant notifications  
- Short-form content  
- Multitasking culture  
- Endless context switching  

Every interruption resets your mental momentum.
