# The Role of Design Systems in Modern Products

As products grow, consistency becomes harder to maintain. That’s where design systems come in.

## What is a design system?

A design system is a collection of:
- Components
- Guidelines
- Patterns

It ensures consistency across the product.

## Benefits

- Faster development  
- Better collaboration between design and engineering  
- Consistent user experience  

## Not just a UI library

A true design system includes:
- Documentation  
- Design principles  
- Usage guidelines  

## Final thoughts

Investing in a design system early can save countless hours as your product scales.
